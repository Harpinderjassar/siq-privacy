Account and Data Deletion

StandardsIQ Pty Ltd · Applies to StandardsIQ Cleaner, SIQ Supervisor and StandardsIQ Admin

How to request deletion of your account and data

StandardsIQ accounts are issued by the organisation you work for or do business with, so there are two ways to make a request. Either works.

  1. Through your organisation. Ask your employer's StandardsIQ administrator to remove your account. This is usually the fastest path.
  2. Directly to us. Email admin@standardsiq.com.au with the subject "Account deletion request" from the email address linked to your account, and name the app you use (StandardsIQ Cleaner, SIQ Supervisor or StandardsIQ Admin). We will verify the request with you and confirm in writing when it is complete, normally within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Your account and sign in credentials.
  • Your profile details: name, email address, phone number and emergency contact details.
  • Your device's push notification tokens.
  • Your messages' link to your identity.

What is kept, and for how long

Some records must be retained by the organisation you worked for to meet Australian legal obligations, even after your account is deleted:

  • Attendance and shift records, retained as employment records for up to 7 years as required by the Fair Work Act.
  • Incident, hazard and safety records, retained as required by work health and safety laws.
  • Compliance document records where the organisation is required to evidence workforce checks.

Where records must be kept, we remove or de-identify personal details that are not required for the record's legal purpose. Photographs of work areas do not identify you and remain part of the service record of the site.
